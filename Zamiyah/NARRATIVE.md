## Editing Rules (Quick Reference)

- **If someone is talking, show them** — even 1-second interjections
- **Cut on the first word**, not after a delay
- **Minimum hold**: 1s for speaking, 2s before cutting to reaction
- **Don't cut for filler** ("mm-hmm", "yeah") — stay on speaker
- **Average pace**: 1 cut every 8-15 seconds
- **Let long stories breathe** — don't cut just because it's been 30s
- **Wide shot = reset** — use it between topics, not during emotional peaks
